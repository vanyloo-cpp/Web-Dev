Greek Pasta Salad

  • Time: 30 min.
  • Serves: 2.
Ingredients Quantity
Penne pasta 300g
Cherry tomatoes 100g

Step 1: In a large bowl, combine all of the salad ingredients and toss together.
Step 2:In another bowl, combine olive oil, balsamic vinegar, Italian seasoning, pepper and salt. Whisk to combine.
Step 3:Pour dressing over salad and stir well to coat. Chill.

Greek Pasta Salad

  • Time: 25 min.
  • Serves: 4.
Ingredients Quantity
White Flour 500g
Fast yeast 7g

Step 1: Mix 500g strong white flour, 2 tsp salt and a 7g sachet of fast-action yeast in a large bowl.
Step 2:Make a well in the centre, then add 3 tbsp olive oil and 300ml water, and mix well.
Step 3:Tip onto a lightly floured work surface and knead for around 10 mins.
